CODEBOOK

The output of run_analysis.R creates the dataset file 'tidy_data.txt', which includes the following variables.

SUBJECT - the identifying number of the subject that generated the data. In total there are 30 subjects, who each performed all six activities.

ACTIVITY - the label of the activity that generated the data. There are six total: WALKING, WALKING_UPSTAIRS, WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS, SITTING, STANDING, LAYING DOWN.

The next 86 variables are mean or standard deviation (marked 'std') numeric values selected from the original 561 features. More information on the naming and source of these variables can be obtained the at the UCI Machine Learning Repository.
